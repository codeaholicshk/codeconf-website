UX, Design, Front-end MVC, Server-side development, Mobile apps, DevOps...

The Speakers

  • Amber Matthews

    Amber is a UX designer and digital strategist/geek plus an unashamed sports nut.

    She has a black belt in martial arts, but says that she doesn't bite. Honest.

  • Andrew Medworth

    Andrew is a British software engineer who has been living in Hong Kong since 2010.

    His interests are programming languages, music, chess, philosophy and Chinese language.

  • Bart Verkoeijen

    Bart is a software developer building apps, web and cloud.

    With over 10 years experience in web development, he's spent the past year working on Surround App.

  • Brendan Tang

    Brendan is a hacker, coder, artist, and open source contributor.

    He's worked in Shanghai and Beijing, and is now co-founder of Innohat in Hong Kong.

  • Hubert Lee

    Hubert Lee is a technologist, a tinkerer, and a traveler.

    He moved to Hong Kong in 2012 to work with Thought Sauce, and has since started dabbling with mobile apps for Android.

  • Jonas S Karlsson

    Jonas used to build the storage and indexing software for Google.

    Now he spends his time travelling between Hackerspaces all over Asia.

  • Matthew Lehner

    Matthew is a front-end perfectionist, back end architect, and all around good guy.

    He's interested in creating simple, reliable software, and simple, reliable businesses.

  • Michelle Sun

    Michelle is co-founder and CEO of First Code Academy.

    She wants to empower the world to use technology to express themselves and bring their ideas to life.

    She started First Code Academy to provide hands-on learning opportunities in software development in Asia.

  • Mu-An Chiou

    Mu-An is a designer at Github. She's also a 台灣人.

  • Pedro Pimentel

    Pedro is a full-stack software & social engineer.

    He can seamlessly switch between talking code and talking with humans thanks to his experience working with consultancies Thoughtworks and ThoughtSauce.

    Originally from Brazil, he came to Asia to change the world and, moreover, change himself.

  • Richard Cohen

    Richard is "Mr Android" in Hong Kong. But he actually spends most of his time working with Python and Django!

  • Sebastian Korfmann

    Sebastian is a seasoned Web-Engineer from Germany, working for Bulletproof Networks in Australia.

    He loves Ruby and Docker. When he's not travelling around the world he's running meet-ups for the startup community in Saigon.
